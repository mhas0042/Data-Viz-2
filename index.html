Summary Findings

Global happiness and well-being are essential concepts reflecting the overall quality of life and contentment experienced by people across the world. Happiness encompasses a state of emotional and mental well-being, often associated with positive feelings, fulfillment, and life satisfaction. Well-being, on the other hand, is a broader term encompassing various aspects of health, prosperity, and happiness in both personal and societal contexts.

Understanding global happiness and well-being involves examining economic stability, social support systems, access to healthcare and education, environmental quality, and levels of personal freedom. Factors such as economic prosperity, social equality, mental and physical health, strong social connections, and a sense of purpose contribute significantly to individual and collective well-being.
